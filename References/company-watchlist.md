## Usage Guidelines

### Competitor Prioritization

**Tier 1 Priority (Track Closely):**
- Hi-Lex, Aisin, Brose, Huf, Magna Closures, Mitsui, Inteva
- Any technology partnerships or new product launches from these players
- OEM sourcing decisions involving these competitors

**Tier 2 Priority (Monitor Regularly):**
- Ushin, Witte, Mitsuba, Tri-Circle, Fudi, PHA, Daedong, Cebi
- Market share shifts, regional expansions, technology upgrades

**Tier 3 Priority (Watch for Disruption):**
- ANy other Tier1 suppliers
- Potential low-cost disruption, Chinese market growth, emerging tech

### OEM Customer Intelligence

**High Priority OEM Developments:**
- New vehicle platform announcements (affects closure specifications)
- EV strategy shifts (electric closures, soft-close, power features)
- Supplier consolidation or diversification strategies
- Smart entry and connectivity roadmaps
- Cost reduction initiatives

**Regional Focus:**
- **North America**: Pickup trucks and SUVs (heavy-duty latches, weathersealing)
- **Europe**: Premium features, regulatory compliance (pedestrian safety, anti-trap)
- **China**: High-tech features, cost competition, local content requirements

### Technology Trend Tracking

**Key Technologies to Monitor:**
- **Hands-free/Phone-as-Key**: UWB, BLE, NFC technologies
- **Soft-close/Power Closures**: Electric actuation, motor technologies
- **Safety Systems**: Anti-pinch, radar sensing, child safety
- **Lightweight Materials**: Aluminum, composites for latch housings
- **Integration**: Door modules, complete closure systems
- **Cybersecurity**: Secure access, digital key vulnerabilities

### Competitive Intelligence Priorities

**Critical Signals:**
1. **New OEM Awards**: Who won what platform? (Scope, value, timeline)
2. **Technology Launches**: New latch designs, smart entry systems, sensor integration
3. **Partnerships**: Technology collaborations (e.g., latch supplier + radar supplier)
4. **Capacity Expansions**: New plants (signals OEM wins or regional strategy)
5. **M&A Activity**: Consolidation in closure systems space
6. **Patents**: Innovative latch mechanisms, electronic integration, safety features

**Red Flags:**
- Competitor wins at your key customers
- Technology leapfrogs (e.g., integrated radar in closures)
- Low-cost Chinese competitors moving upmarket
- OEM vertical integration (bringing closure systems in-house)
- Cybersecurity vulnerabilities in smart entry systems
