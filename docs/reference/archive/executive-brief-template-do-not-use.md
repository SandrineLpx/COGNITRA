## Template Usage Guidelines

### Weekly Brief
- **Length**: 4-8 pages
- **Audience**: Working-level managers and analysts
- **Focus**: Tactical intelligence, immediate actions
- **Timing**: Every Friday end-of-day or Monday morning
- **Detail level**: Moderate to high

### Monthly Brief
- **Length**: 10-20 pages
- **Audience**: Senior executives and strategic planners
- **Focus**: Strategic trends, competitive positioning
- **Timing**: First week of following month
- **Detail level**: High-level synthesis with strategic depth

### Customization Points

**Adapt sections based on:**
- **Audience needs**: Remove appendices for senior execs, expand for analysts
- **Activity level**: If slow week, combine topics; if busy, expand key topics
- **Focus areas**: Emphasize topics most relevant to your organization
- **Regional focus**: Adapt for specific geographic markets

**Optional sections (add as needed):**
- Patent Deep Dive (if significant IP activity)
- Regional Focus (if concentrating on specific market)
- Technology Deep Dive (for specific technology watch)
- M&A Activity Tracker (if significant consolidation)
- Startup Spotlight (if tracking emerging players)

### Quality Checklist

Before finalizing brief:
- [ ] Executive Summary is clear and actionable
- [ ] High priority items have strategic context, not just facts
- [ ] Trends are supported by multiple data points
- [ ] Recommendations are specific and assigned
- [ ] All links and references are working
- [ ] Company names are consistent and accurate
- [ ] Metrics are calculated correctly
- [ ] Sources are properly attributed
- [ ] Formatting is clean and professional
- [ ] Length is appropriate for audience

### Distribution Best Practices

**SharePoint:**
- Save to `03_Executive Briefs/Weekly/` or `Monthly/` folder
- Use consistent naming: `Week_XX_MonDD-DD.docx` or `Month_YYYY.docx`
- Tag with appropriate metadata

**Teams:**
- Post link to brief in Market Intelligence channel
- Include 2-3 sentence teaser highlighting top items
- @mention stakeholders for high-priority items

**Email:**
- Send PDF version to executive distribution list
- Include executive summary in email body
- Attach full brief
- Link to SharePoint for archive

### Evolution

This template should be refined over time:
- Gather feedback from stakeholders on format and content
- Track which sections get most engagement
- Adjust length and detail based on actual usage
- Add/remove sections as intelligence priorities shift
